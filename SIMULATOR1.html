How MethanoMine Works

  1. Capture methane from dung pits, covered lagoons and rice paddies.
  2. Transport gas to MethanoPod via low-pressure piping.
  3. Convert using microbe reactors or burn as biogas for electricity/cooking.
  4. Use biomass as compost/fish feed and monetize carbon credits.

MethanoPod — Features

  • Modular, solar-powered control unit
  • Gas sensors & remote IoT telemetry
  • Plug-and-play piping and skids
  • Safe flame arrestors & pressure relief
